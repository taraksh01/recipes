Lemon Drizzle Cake

Description

This lemon drizzle cake is one of the tastiest and easiest cakes ever This is a grat recipe for someone who doesn't like too many Steps

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 lemon, zested
  • 2 1/4 cups self raising flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk

Lemon Drizzle Topping:

  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Add sugar to a mixing bowl and grate in lemon zest. Stir and let sit for 1 hour if possible to intesify the lemon flavour. This step can be skipped if necessary.
  2. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C. Line a loaf pan with greased parchment paper.
  3. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a small bowl; add to lemon sugar. Add butter, eggs, ad milk and beat with and electric mixer until batter is smooth and fluffy. Transfer batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth out the top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 50 minutes. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile for lemon drizzle topping whisk sugar and lemon juice together in a bowl until smooth.
  6. Poke holes all over the cake with skewer, and ladle or pour lemon drizzle topping over the top. Let the cake cool all the way to room temperature before removing it from the pan.
